TOP 10 CELEBRITY IN TECH WORLD

sundar pichai

Sunder Pichai's journey in Google started in the year 2004 where he began working on trendy products, such as Google Toolbar, Google Gears, and Google Pack before he launched the most popular Google Chrome. Yes you read right, Sunder Pichai was the man behind Google Chrome, and we really wanna thank him. He initially worked on the Google Toolbar, which enabled those using the Microsoft Internet Explorer and Mozilla Firefox Web browsers to easily access the Google search engine. Over the next few years, he was directly involved in the development of Google's own browser, Chrome, which was released to the public in 2008.
