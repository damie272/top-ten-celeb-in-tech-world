satya nadella

Satya Nadella (born August 19, 1967, Hyderabad, India) is an Indian-born business executive who was CEO of the computer software company Microsoft from 2014. Nadella grew up in the southern Indian city of Hyderabad and studied electrical engineering at Mangalore University (B.Sc., 1988). He is the executive chairman and CEO of Microsoft, succeeding Steve Ballmer in 2014 as CEO and John W. Thompson in 2021 as chairman. Before becoming CEO, he was the executive vice president of Microsoft's cloud and enterprise group, responsible for building and running the company's computing platforms. Nadella's leadership philosophy focuses on empathy, innovation, and a growth mindset, which are cornerstones of his approach to revitalizing the company.
